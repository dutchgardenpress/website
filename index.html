Our Authors

Kate Somers


Kate Somers has loved historical fiction for as long as she can remember. An avid reader and writer of fiction, she took a novel-writing course in 2020 and soon after wrote The Bristol Scholar. Kate aims to incorporate real, raw, and relatable characters into her writing, drawing on her own experiences to connect with her readers. You can keep up to date with her writing by signing up for her newsletter at katesomers.com
